This task in not possible without holding some value in memory, either count of cards
or value of the card(If all cards are unique).

We check first two cards. Compare them and largest one goes bottom of the deck also we register this value.
We do this operation until we reach registered card. We have our initial registered card and smallest card in deck.
We put the smallest card at bottom of the deck.
Repeat the operation until we have a sorted deck.
